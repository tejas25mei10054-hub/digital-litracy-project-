# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO's ✅

1. **Do keep your profiles professional.** Use a clear, appropriate profile photo and a bio that reflects your academic and career interests. Employers and recruiters do check social media.

2. **Do think before you post.** Ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable if my professor, parent, or future employer saw this?" If not, do not post it.

3. **Do use strong, unique passwords** for every social media account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever available.

4. **Do report harmful content.** If you see cyberbullying, hate speech, misinformation, or suspicious behaviour, use the platform's reporting tools.

5. **Do adjust your privacy settings.** Regularly review what information is visible to the public versus friends or connections only.

6. **Do give credit.** When sharing someone else's content — images, articles, videos — always credit the original creator.

7. **Do use social media to learn and network.** Follow researchers, professionals, and organisations in your field to stay updated and grow your knowledge.

---

## DON'Ts ❌

1. **Don't share personal information publicly.** Never post your phone number, home address, Aadhaar number, bank details, or daily routine on any public platform.

2. **Don't share OTPs, PINs, or passwords** with anyone, even if they claim to be customer support or a trusted friend.

3. **Don't engage with or spread misinformation.** Verify news through credible sources before sharing. Spreading fake news can have serious legal and social consequences.

4. **Don't participate in or ignore cyberbullying.** Do not bully others online, and do not stay silent if you witness it happening to someone else.

5. **Don't accept friend/follow requests from strangers** without verifying their identity, especially on platforms where your personal information is visible.

6. **Don't post anything in frustration or anger.** Impulsive posts made in emotion are often regretted later and can damage your personal and professional reputation permanently.
