# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Tool Used
**Canva** (canva.com) – Free online design tool

## What My Infographic Shows

The infographic is titled **"Digital Literacy: Your Superpower in the Digital Age"** and covers the following three topics:

1. **What is Digital Literacy?**
   A brief definition explaining that digital literacy is the ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies responsibly and effectively.

2. **Safe Internet Practices**
   Tips such as: using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, not sharing personal information online, and identifying phishing attempts.

3. **Professional Online Presence**
   Guidance on building a clean digital footprint — including LinkedIn, GitHub, and keeping social media content professional.

## Reflection

While creating this infographic on Canva, I found the drag-and-drop interface very intuitive and easy to use, even without any prior design experience. I chose icons and a colour scheme (blue and white) that felt clean and professional. The most interesting part was figuring out how to condense complex ideas like digital literacy into short, punchy phrases that would make sense at a glance. The most difficult part was deciding what NOT to include — there was so much content I wanted to add, but keeping it to one page meant I had to prioritise only the most important points. This exercise made me realise how powerful visual communication can be when done well.
